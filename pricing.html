Everything you need to know
Do I need a credit card to start the free trial?
No. The 15-day free trial requires no credit card and no commitment. You get full platform access immediately after signup.
What happens after the free trial ends?
Your account moves to a limited free tier. Your data is preserved. You can upgrade at any time to restore full access — nothing is deleted.
Is there a minimum seat requirement?
No. You can start with a single seat on any plan. This is one of our core differentiators — every competitor requires 2–10 seats minimum.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime with no penalty. Annual plans include a 14-day money-back guarantee from the date of purchase.
What is the refund policy?
We offer a full refund within 14 days of your initial subscription. Renewal charges are non-refundable. Contact support@retentionschematicsos.com for refund requests.
How is the pricing 15% below market rate?
We benchmarked against 10 competitors including Custify, Totango, HubSpot Service Hub, and ClientSuccess. Our Starter and Pro prices are set 15% below the weighted average comparable rate at each tier. The full research is available on request.
Does the platform support Arabic or MENA-specific workflows?
The platform includes MENA-specific benchmark data, Majlis premium CAC inputs, and retention models calibrated for MENA SaaS and fintech dynamics. Arabic language UI is on the roadmap.
How do Team plan discounts work?
Team pricing applies automatically based on seat count. Team S (2–4 seats): $98/seat. Team M (5–10 seats): $93/seat. Team L (11+ seats): $87/seat. Annual plans add an additional 16.7% savings on top of the volume discount. Contact us for custom quotes above 20 seats.
